Property Overview

Enjoy both the ocean view with the sunrise and the tidal march with the sunset from this home on Glades Road just a minute walk from 2 beaches. Porches on 3 sides. Outdoor shower. Offstreet parking. The street has no parking so day trippers to the beach can not park, so it is never crowded. The same families have owned or rented here for years.

Train to Boston is a 10 minute drive away and it takes about 56 minutes. Scituate Harbor and Cohasset are about a 10 minute drive.
